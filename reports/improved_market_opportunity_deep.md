*   **Too Early:**
    *   **Regulatory Uncertainty:** Lack of clear, harmonized global regulations around digital asset inheritance could pose challenges.

    *   **Trust Building:** A new company in this sensitive space needs significant time and resources to build unshakeable trust.
*   **Too Late:**
    *   Highly unlikely. While some niche solutions exist (e.g., Bitcoin-only inheritance, generic digital vaults), a comprehensive, multi-chain, zero-knowledge, and legally-aware "dead man's switch" for crypto is still a nascent market. No dominant player has emerged.
