LegacyGuard is poised to become the definitive security infrastructure layer for crypto inheritance, addressing a critical and growing $1.977 billion Serviceable Addressable Market. ### The Problem (Quantified)

The burgeoning cryptocurrency market, with over 659 million global owners, faces a silent but critical challenge: **the secure and reliable inheritance of digital assets.** Millions of dollars in crypto are lost or become permanently inaccessible each year due to death or incapacitation. This issue creates immense emotional distress for families and acts as a significant barrier to mainstream crypto adoption. Our analysis indicates that **131.8 million crypto owners** are concerned about inheritance, representing a **$13.18 billion Total Addressable Market (TAM)** annually. The lack of a trusted, user-friendly solution for digital asset estate planning is a fundamental flaw in the digital wealth transfer ecosystem.

### The Solution & Differentiator

LegacyGuard is a secure inheritance protocol and security infrastructure layer that facilitates the transfer of encrypted crypto wallet recovery instructions to designated heirs upon the owner's inactivity or death.

**Core Solution:**
*   **Zero-Knowledge Security:** All sensitive recovery instructions are client-side encrypted using a user-generated Master Recovery Key (MRK). LegacyGuard never has access to unencrypted user data or private keys.
*   **Dead Man's Switch Mechanism:** Configurable check-in frequencies, grace periods, and multi-channel reminders ensure timely activation.
*   **Multi-Factor Heir Verification:** Robust KYC and optional additional verification steps for heirs to ensure secure and compliant access.
*   **Guided Recovery:** An empathetic, step-by-step process for heirs to securely decrypt and utilize recovery instructions.

**Key Differentiators:**
1.  **Zero-Knowledge by Design:** Unwavering commitment to privacy and self-custody, distinguishing us from solutions that require custodianship.
2.  **Comprehensive & Chain-Agnostic:** Focuses on instructions for *any* wallet or chain, rather than direct on-chain asset transfer, offering broader utility.
3.  **Intuitive & Empathetic UX:** Designed for both the "Legacy Creator" (simple setup) and "Legacy Receiver" (compassionate recovery), addressing the human element of inheritance.
4.  **Proactive Legal Compliance:** Positioned as a complementary tool to traditional estate planning, with clear disclaimers and robust verification processes to navigate complex legal landscapes.
5.  **Security Infrastructure Layer:** Designed to integrate with wallets, custody solutions, and estate-planning stakeholders, positioning LegacyGuard as an essential utility.
