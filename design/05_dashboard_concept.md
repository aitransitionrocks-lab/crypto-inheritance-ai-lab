# Dashboard UI Concept & Partner/Developer Interface
*LegacyGuard Product Design — Generated 2026-03-17*

---

LegacyGuard's design philosophy centers on translating complex security and inheritance concepts into an intuitive, trustworthy, and simple user experience. We aim for an "Apple-level" product clarity, ensuring users feel in control and understand every step of their digital legacy planning.

---

## Design Principles Applied:

*   **Trust & Security First:** Prominent display of security status, clear alerts, and explicit communication of LegacyGuard's non-custodial nature.
*   **Clarity & Simplicity:** Minimal jargon, clean UI, logical information hierarchy, and guided workflows.
*   **Control & Transparency:** Users always know the status of their plans, who their heirs are, and when actions are required.
*   **Accessibility:** Thoughtful use of color, typography, and interactive elements.
*   **Scalability:** Component-based design for easy expansion and maintenance across B2C and B2B interfaces.

---
